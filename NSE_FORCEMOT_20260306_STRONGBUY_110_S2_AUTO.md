## PHASE 1: BUSINESS ORIENTATION (20 Points)

### 1.1 Business Model Clarity — 4/5

Force Motors is a fully vertically integrated automobile manufacturer established in 1958 (formerly Bajaj Tempo). Revenue streams include:

- **Light Commercial Vehicles (LCV):** Traveller platform — 70%+ domestic LCV passenger market share. Used for tourism, education, healthcare, institutional transport.
- **Multi-Utility Vehicles (MUV):** Urbania — ground-up modular monocoque van platform for shared urban mobility. Trax for rural/last-mile transport.
- **Special/Defence Vehicles:** Gurkha SUV (Light Strike Vehicle) for Indian Army/Air Force. 2,978-unit defence order received March 2025.
- **Automotive Components:** Engine assembly for BMW India and Mercedes-Benz India — longstanding OEM relationships.
- **Sports Utility Vehicles (SUV):** Gurkha civilian variant — niche off-road segment.

Segment-wise revenue split not fully broken out in public filings (LCV/MUV combined), but product lines are clearly documented. Business model is differentiated through vertical integration and niche dominance.

**Source:** BSE Annual Report FY25, Screener.in, CRISIL rating rationale Jul 2025
