## PHASE 1: BUSINESS ORIENTATION (20 Points)

### 1.1 Business Model Clarity — Score: 5/5

CG Power operates through two clearly defined segments:

**Power Systems** (~45% of standalone revenue): Manufactures power and distribution transformers, EHV/MV circuit breakers, switchgear, instrument transformers, lightning arrestors, isolators. Also offers EPC/turnkey solutions for T&D substations.

**Industrial Systems** (~55% of standalone revenue): Low-voltage (LT) motors (40% market share in India), medium-voltage motors, AC generators, drives & automation, railway traction equipment (traction motors, electronics, signaling via subsidiary GG Tronics), and stampings.

**New Segment — Semiconductors** (via CG Semi Private Ltd): JV with Renesas Electronics and Stars Microelectronics for India's first end-to-end OSAT facility in Sanand, Gujarat. ₹7,600 Cr investment over 5 years. G1 pilot operational; G2 (14.5M units/day capacity) completion by end CY2026.

Revenue model: Product sales + EPC project revenue + service/maintenance. Capital-intensive in power transformers; asset-light in motors/drives distribution. Export contribution growing (50% YoY growth in 9M FY26).

*Source: Screener.in, Q2 FY26 Concall Transcript, Company Website*

### 1.2 Industry Positioning — Score: 4/5
