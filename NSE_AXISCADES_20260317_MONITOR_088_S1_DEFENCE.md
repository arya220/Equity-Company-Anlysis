## PHASE 1: BUSINESS ORIENTATION — 20 Points

### 1.1 Business Model Clarity — 5 pts

Axiscades operates through two reportable segments:

1. **Technology Services and Solutions (TSS):** Engineering design services, R&D, and product lifecycle solutions across aerospace, automotive, heavy engineering, and energy.
2. **Strategic Technology Solutions (STS):** Defence electronics, system integration, avionics, radar/sonar processing via subsidiary Mistral Solutions.

**Revenue segmentation (Q2 FY26):** Defence 38%, Aerospace 30%, ESAI 11%, HE & Auto 18%, Energy 4%. Core verticals (Defence + Aero + ESAI) = 78% of revenue.

**Revenue model:** Mix of services (time & material contracts) and product/solutions (fixed-price defence orders). Transitioning to 61:39 product:services mix by FY27.

**Geographical:** India, Europe, USA, Canada, APAC.

Sources: Screener.in, CARE rating report (Oct 2025), TradeBrains (Nov 2025 segment breakdown), Q3 FY26 earnings highlights.
