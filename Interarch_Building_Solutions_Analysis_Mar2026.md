## PHASE 1: BUSINESS ORIENTATION — 14/20

### 1.1 Business Model Clarity — 4/5

Interarch is one of India's leading turnkey pre-engineered steel construction solution providers, ranked #2 by PEB operating revenue in FY25 among integrated Indian players. The company operates across three segments:

- **PEB Systems (~85% of FY25 revenue):** Design, engineering, manufacturing, supply, and erection of pre-engineered steel buildings for industrial, infrastructure, and commercial applications. Customers include Ather Energy, Tata Motors JLR, M&M, Balkrishna Industries, Addverb Technologies, Grasim Industries.
- **Building Products (~15% of FY25 revenue, ₹221 Cr):** Metal roofing/cladding (TRACDEK brand, since 1993) and suspended ceiling systems (TRAC brand, since 1983).
- **Heavy Steel Structures (new vertical, nascent):** Extra-heavy steel fabrication (5–15 ton sections) for data centers, semiconductor fabs, renewables, stadiums. Dedicated 24,000 MT plant in AP targeted live by Q2 FY27.

Business model is asset-light with high asset turnover (~₹450–500 Cr revenue per ₹50–60 Cr capex). Repeat orders constitute 80–85% of order book, indicating strong customer stickiness.

*Source: Screener.in company description; BSE filings; Q1 FY26 investor presentation; Mangal Keshav research report*

🔒 **LOCK Score: 4/5**

### 1.2 Industry Positioning — 4/5

Interarch holds the #2 position among integrated PEB players in India by FY25 operating revenue (behind Kirby Building Systems, a Nucor subsidiary). EBITDA margins of 8–10% are in line with sector median. The company differentiates through:
