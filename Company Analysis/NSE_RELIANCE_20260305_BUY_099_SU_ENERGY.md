## PHASE 1: BUSINESS ORIENTATION (20 Points)

### 1.1 Business Model Clarity — 🔒 Score: 5/5

RIL operates across five clearly documented segments with % contribution disclosed each quarter:

- **Oil-to-Chemicals (O2C):** ~55% of revenue. World's largest single-site refinery complex at Jamnagar (68.2 MMTPA). Integrated refining + petrochemicals. Revenue Q3 FY26: ₹1,62,000 Cr.
- **Digital Services (Jio):** ~15% of revenue. India's largest telecom operator, 515.3 Mn subscribers, 253 Mn on 5G, ARPU ₹213.7. Revenue Q3 FY26: ₹43,683 Cr.
- **Retail:** ~33% of revenue. India's largest retailer, 19,979 stores, 378 Mn registered customers. Revenue Q3 FY26: ₹97,605 Cr.
- **Oil & Gas (E&P):** ~2% of revenue. KG-D6 block (~30% of India's domestic gas production). Revenue Q3 FY26: ₹5,833 Cr.
- **New Energy:** Pre-revenue. Solar giga-factory operational (200 MW HJT modules produced). Battery (40 GWh) and electrolyzer (3 GW) gigafactories commissioning 2026.
- **Media (JioStar):** Disney-Star JV. Revenue Q3 FY26: ₹8,010 Cr. 450 Mn MAUs.

*Source: Q3 FY26 Investor Presentation, ril.com; Screener.in consolidated quarterly results*

### 1.2 Industry Positioning — 🔒 Score: 4/5

- O2C: Among the world's top 3 private-sector refining operations. Jamnagar has consistent cost advantage through deep conversion capability and integrated petrochemicals.
- Jio: Market leader in Indian telecom by subscribers and revenue market share. 5G network accounts for 53% of wireless traffic.
- Retail: At least 3x larger than the next nearest retailer by revenue; FMCG arm (₹4,400 Cr quarterly run-rate) expanding rapidly with Campa beverages reaching ~14% market share in some geographies.
- Deducted 1 point: Consolidated EBITDA margin at ~17% is in-line with diversified conglomerate norms but not top-quartile for any individual segment versus pure-play peers (Jio Platforms EBITDA margin ~54% is top-tier, but O2C and Retail margins are sector-median).

*Source: Q1 FY26 concall transcript (ril.com), ICRA rating rationale Jan 2026*
