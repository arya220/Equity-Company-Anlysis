## PHASE 1: BUSINESS ORIENTATION — 17/20

### 1.1 Business Model Clarity — 4/5

Transrail Lighting is an integrated EPC company focused on power transmission & distribution (T&D). T&D constitutes ~90% of the order book. Additional verticals include civil construction, poles & lighting, and railways.

Revenue split: ~53% international (across 63 countries, 15 in Africa), ~47% domestic. Key clients include Power Grid Corporation of India, Adani, Renew Power, and multilateral-funded international projects.

Backward-integrated manufacturing: towers (1,01,000 TPA), conductors (60,000 TPA), poles (25,000 TPA). Brownfield expansion underway to double tower capacity to 1,96,000 TPA.

Segment-level revenue breakout is available at the T&D vs non-T&D level. Detailed sub-segment contribution percentages within non-T&D verticals are less granular.

*Score: 4/5 — Clear model with documented revenue streams; minor segments not fully broken out.*
*Source: Screener.in, Q2 FY26 concall transcript, CRISIL rating report*

EBITDA margins: Transrail consistently delivers 12–15% EBITDA margins vs KEC International (~8%) and Kalpataru Projects (~8–9%). This is the best margin profile in the listed T&D EPC peer group.

The company's 40-year track record and presence in 63 countries provide structural differentiation from smaller/newer entrants.
