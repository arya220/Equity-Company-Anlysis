### 0.5.3 Global Precedent Check — **1/2**
**Precedents:** China (2000s–2010s thermal buildout — Shanghai Electric, Dongfang); South Korea (Doosan); USA (1960s–1980s).

India is ~10–15 years behind China's thermal expansion peak.

**KEY DIFFERENCES (mandatory):**
- India has weaker DISCOMs (DISCOM payment risk persistent)
- More land acquisition/environmental clearance friction
- Smaller individual project scale vs China's mega-projects
- More fragmented policy execution at state level
- Indian market more concentrated (BHEL dominates) vs China's competitive market
- India's per-capita coal consumption far lower; addressable demand structurally smaller

🔒 **Score: 1/2** | Source: Multi-source synthesis
