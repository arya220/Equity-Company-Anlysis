The company recently secured exclusive distribution rights for Novo Nordisk's semaglutide (Poviztra) in India — a potentially transformative partnership in the GLP-1/obesity space.

However, **semaglutide patent expired in India in March 2026**, with 50+ generic brands launching at 50-80% discounts. At CMP ₹1,594 (P/E 32x), the stock is priced for perfection with negative probability-weighted expected return. Verdict: **MONITOR** — wait for entry at ₹1,100-1,250 where risk-reward becomes favorable.

### 2. Thematic Context

**Theme:** India's chronic therapy shift and GLP-1/obesity treatment penetration
