## PHASE 1: BUSINESS ORIENTATION — 🔒 18/20

### 1.1 Business Model Clarity — 5/5
Blue Star operates across three clearly documented segments:
- **Segment I — Electro-Mechanical Projects & Commercial AC (EMP):** 50% of FY25 revenue (₹5,998 Cr). Turnkey HVAC/MEP projects for data centers, factories, hospitals, commercial buildings. Revenue grew 27.2% YoY in FY25. EBIT margin 8.2%.
- **Segment II — Unitary Products (UP):** 47% of FY25 revenue (₹5,621 Cr). Room ACs, commercial refrigeration, water purifiers, air coolers. Revenue grew 22.4% YoY. EBIT margin 8.4%.
- **Segment III — Professional Electronics & Industrial Systems (PEIS):** 3% of FY25 revenue (₹349 Cr). Technology solutions, MedTech, data security. Revenue declined 7.7%.

Monetization is transparent: project-based billing for EMP, B2C/B2B product sales for UP, distribution/services for PEIS. Capital structure is project-working-capital funded (EMP) and channel-funded (UP).

*Source: CARE AA+ rating rationale Jul 2025, Screener.in annual data*

### 1.2 Industry Positioning — 4/5
Blue Star holds leadership positions across multiple product categories: #1 in ducted AC systems and scroll chillers, #2/3 in VRF and screw chillers, 14% RAC market share, and 35% commercial refrigeration market share. Distribution network spans 8,800+ stores across 650+ locations.

EBITDA margins at 7% are moderate relative to the industry — constrained by the competitive RAC segment and imported component dependency. However, ROCE at 26% (FY25) is meaningfully superior to peers like Voltas (~8-12% ROE). Blue Star is differentiated but operates in a price-competitive market.

*Source: CARE rating Jul 2025, Screener.in*

### 1.3 Recent Developments — 4/5
Significant positive developments (12 months):
- **Sri City Phase II operational (Feb 2025):** Total capacity now 7.7 lakh units/annum.
- **Sri City Phase III under construction:** Target completion end-FY26, raising capacity to 12.3 lakh units.
- **125 new AC models launched for Summer 2026**, compliant with new BEE energy labels (effective Jan 1, 2026).
- **Scalable production capacity: 1.4M units, expandable to 1.8M units** for rising demand.
- **Order book at ₹6,899 Cr** (Dec 2025), up 1.3% QoQ.
- **FY25 revenue crossed ₹12,000 Cr** for the first time.

Negative: **Cybersecurity incident** (Jan 31, 2026) — unauthorized access to product installation data. Investigation ongoing per BSE filing (Feb 1, 2026). This is product data, not financial data — low severity but flagged.

*Source: BSE filings, Q3 FY26 earnings summary, CARE rating*
