## 8. KEY RISKS

1. **US growth muted**: Q3 FY26 US revenue flat at $70M vs same quarter last year. Delayed flu season, slower controlled substance quota allocations, increased competition on some products. $400M by FY28 requires meaningful acceleration from $280M current run-rate.
2. **Pledging still elevated**: 30.6% is a significant improvement but remains above 25% comfort level. Creates stock price vulnerability during market sell-offs through forced liquidation risk.
3. **Regulatory risk**: USFDA Form 483 (4 observations) at Chestnut Ridge was procedural and closed as VAI, but historical 2019 warning letter for Puducherry plant (data integrity) is a reputational overhang. Pharmaceutical industry inherently subject to regulatory action.
4. **Senior management attrition**: CHRO resigned (Jan 2026), Puneet Malik (Sr. Management) left for Aditya Birla Group. While not C-suite, pattern of senior departures bears monitoring.
5. **Working capital elongation**: Cash-to-cash cycle increasing due to longer working capital cycle in ex-US markets. Debtor days 96 (FY25) — better than FY23-24 but cash conversion cycle at 141 days is elevated.
