## 4. KEY RISKS

1. **Client and sector concentration**: Top 5 clients = 56% of order book; T&D + FGD = ~90% of order book. Any slowdown in government T&D spending directly impacts revenue. *(Source: ICRA July 2025)*

2. **Data center execution risk**: ₹8,000 Cr long-term DC investment plan is large. Mumbai DC delayed due to land issues. Any debt-funded acceleration strains the AA rating. DC track record in asset-heavy new businesses unproven. *(Source: ICRA July 2025; Q3 FY26 concall)*

3. **AMI counterparty risk**: 2.2 million smart meter orders (₹2,612 Cr) involve weak state discoms. Upfront capex recovered over 10-year annuity. Escrow arrangements mitigate but don't eliminate. *(Source: ICRA July 2025)*

4. **High other income dependence**: ₹205 Cr other income in FY25 = 39% of PBT. As cash deploys into DC capex, other income reduces and reported EPS may appear to "decline" even if operations improve. *(Source: Screener FY25 P&L)*

5. **Working capital intensity at scale**: Receivables of 200–280 days in FY22–FY23 caused FY24 CFO to turn severely negative despite EBITDA growth. A repeat at ₹3,400 Cr revenue scale would be materially worse. *(Source: ICRA July 2025; Equitymaster FY24 analysis)*
