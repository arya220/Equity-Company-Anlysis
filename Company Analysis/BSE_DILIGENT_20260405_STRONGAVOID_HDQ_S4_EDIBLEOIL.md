## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Diligent Industries is a micro-cap (₹53 Cr) edible oil manufacturer, processor, and trader based in Denduluru, Andhra Pradesh. Originally incorporated in 1995 as Yatish Securities (an NBFC), the company pivoted to edible oils in 2012–14 after merging with Adithya Agro Allied Oils. Revenue has grown from ₹81 Cr (FY20) to ₹144 Cr (FY25), but the business generates persistently **negative operating cash flow** — CFO has been negative in 4 of the last 5 years, with CFO/EBITDA ratios of -45%, -402%, -144%, and -218% from FY22 through FY25. A massive ₹49.40 Cr rights issue in Nov 2024 (6:5 ratio, >100% dilution) was deployed primarily to repay ₹24.75 Cr in unsecured loans from promoters, with the balance for working capital. **Two Hard DQ triggers fire: (1) CFO/EBITDA negative for 3+ consecutive years, and (2) equity dilution >20% without proportionate EBITDA growth.** Verdict: **STRONG AVOID**.

## 2. THEMATIC CONTEXT (Phase 0.5 — Informational Only)

**Theme:** India edible oil market — refining, processing, and distribution of rice bran oil, palm oil, and castor oil.

**Maturity:** India's edible oil market is mature and highly fragmented. Organized share is significant but dominated by large players (Adani Wilmar, Ruchi Soya/Patanjali, Emami Agrotech). India imports ~55–60% of its edible oil needs, making the sector highly sensitive to global commodity prices and import policy.

**Policy:** The NMEO-OP (National Mission on Edible Oils – Oil Palm) provides targeted support for oil palm cultivation. Diligent was recently allotted 9 mandals in Andhra Pradesh under this scheme (BSE filing, Apr 2026). However, oil palm plantation to harvest takes 4–5 years for meaningful output, and the scale is immaterial relative to the company's current operations.

**Global Precedent:** No relevant consolidation precedent. Edible oil refining/trading is globally fragmented with thin margins. Scale and procurement advantages drive profitability — Diligent has neither.

**Theme Score: 3/12 — No Thematic Tailwind.** Commodity sector, mature market, no structural inflection, thin margins, massive import dependence. The NMEO-OP oil palm allocation is multi-year with uncertain returns.

> ⛔ **Anti-Narrative Rule confirmed:** The oil palm mandal announcement does not constitute a material earnings trigger and cannot override any Hard DQ.
