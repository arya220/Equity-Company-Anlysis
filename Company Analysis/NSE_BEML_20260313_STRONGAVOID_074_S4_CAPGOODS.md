### 1.1 Business Model Clarity — 4/5

Revenue segmentation available in annual reports. PSU model with GoI as 54% promoter. Revenue model is project-based with lumpy Q4 skew. Monetization clear but capital-intensive and working-capital heavy.

*Source: BEML Annual Report FY25, Q3 FY26 Investor Presentation*

*Source: Screener.in peer comparison, CARE rating rationale Jul 2025*
