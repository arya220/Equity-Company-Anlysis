## 🔑 FRAMEWORK LEARNINGS

1. **Not every drawdown is an EDDP opportunity.** The −30% drop in ABB looked tempting but was earnings-driven, not event-driven. The framework's strict Type 1/Type 2 distinction saved capital here.

2. **Premium quality names can still fail the PW gate decisively.** ABB has AAA-quality credit, 22% ROE, 30% ROCE, parent R&D access — and still is −27% PW return at CMP because valuation is untethered from earnings trajectory.

3. **Watch for profit declines masking as "cyclical."** A Tier 1 theme + debt-free balance sheet + 22% ROE is NOT sufficient to justify 76x P/E. The earnings decline is the signal.

4. **RPT risk in MNC subsidiaries:** ABB, Siemens, Schneider all have significant parent-related royalties and transactions. If doing a full analysis, RPT% vs revenue must be verified from AR.
