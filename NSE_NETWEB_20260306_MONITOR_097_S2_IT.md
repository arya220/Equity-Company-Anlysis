## PHASE 1: BUSINESS ORIENTATION (20 Points)

### 1.1 Business Model Clarity — 4/5

Netweb Technologies designs, manufactures, and deploys high-end computing solutions (HCS) in India under the "Tyrone" brand. Revenue streams are documented across segments:

- **HPC / Supercomputing** — Bespoke systems; 500+ installations; appeared 15 times in global Top 500 supercomputers ranking
- **Private Cloud & HCI** — Enterprise private cloud, hybrid cloud, cloud-native storage
- **AI Systems & Enterprise Workstations** — GPU-based AI infrastructure, ML/DL workstations; contributed **64% of Q3 FY26 revenue** (driven by ₹450 Cr strategic order)
- **High Performance Storage / Data Centre Servers**
- **Software & Services** — Skylus.ai GPU orchestration platform, managed services

Revenue split: AI systems ~48% of 9M FY26 revenue (up from 7% two years ago); Govt customers ~59.5%, Non-Govt ~40.5%. Fully integrated design + manufacturing in Faridabad, Haryana. Capital-light model — near-zero debt.

Minor deduction: Exact segment-wise % contribution not consistently broken out in quarterly results beyond broad categories.

🔒 **Score: 4/5** | Source: FY25 AR, Q3 FY26 Concall, Q1 FY26 Investor Presentation

Netweb is India's **only end-to-end Indian-origin OEM** in high-end computing with design and manufacturing capabilities. Key differentiators:

- **NVIDIA OEM Partner** — Early access to latest chips/architectures 12-24 months ahead; also partners with Intel, AMD
- **PLI beneficiary** — Under IT Hardware and Networking PLI schemes
