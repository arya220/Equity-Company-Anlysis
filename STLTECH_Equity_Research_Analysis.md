## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Sterlite Technologies is India's largest backward-integrated optical fiber manufacturer positioned at the center of a structural demand cycle driven by AI data center connectivity, US BEAD broadband, and BharatNet Phase III. Post-demerger (Mar 2025), the company is a cleaner optical networking pure-play with improving working capital and surging order intake (+40% YoY to Rs 4,263Cr). However, the company remains loss-making, has a mixed management track record, and the stock has rallied ~340% from Rs 41 — pricing in significant recovery before profitability is demonstrated. At CMP Rs 183, the stock trades above our base case FY27 target of Rs 152. MONITOR — wait for pullback to Rs 110-130 and PAT-positive confirmation.
